About CyberShield Pakistan

Who we are, why we started, and what we are building.

Our story

CyberShield Pakistan was started because of a simple and painful observation — people around us were suffering from cyberbullying, picture leaking, account hacking, and online blackmail, and they had nowhere safe to go. Reporting to authorities felt impossible. Finding legal help felt out of reach. And the shame that victims carried was something no one should ever have to carry alone.

We started as a single volunteer in Rawalpindi with a Gmail account and a mission. We believe that every Pakistani victim of online abuse deserves a safe, simple, and confidential path to information, support, and justice — regardless of their income, education, or location.

We are currently in our awareness phase, educating the public about cybercrime rights under PECA 2016 and building the partnerships with authorities and support organizations that will power our full complaint system. We are building this slowly and carefully because the people who will use it deserve a system that actually works.

Our mission

Why we exist

To ensure every Pakistani victim of online abuse has a safe, accessible, and confidential path to information, support, and justice — completely free.

Our vision

What we are working toward

A Pakistan where no one suffers online harassment in silence because they did not know their rights or did not know where to turn.

What we stand for

🔒

Confidentiality above everything

Every person who comes to us deserves complete privacy. We will never share any information about anyone who contacts us without their explicit knowledge and permission.

❤

Dignity for every victim

Online abuse causes real harm. We treat every person who comes to us with full respect, zero judgment, and complete sensitivity regardless of their situation.

⚖

Honesty about what we are

We are a volunteer initiative, not a government agency or registered NGO yet. We will always be honest about what we can and cannot do. We make no promises we cannot keep.

👥

Community before everything

We exist for the people of Pakistan, not for recognition or funding. Every decision we make is based on what is best for the victims who come to us.

Important — please read

CyberShield Pakistan is an independent volunteer-led initiative. We are not yet registered as an NGO and are not affiliated with any government body, law enforcement agency, or official authority. We operate on the basis of community service and direct all cases requiring official action to the appropriate registered authorities including FIA Cyber Crime Wing, PTA, Rozan Pakistan, and Digital Rights Foundation. We make no guarantee of any legal outcome.
